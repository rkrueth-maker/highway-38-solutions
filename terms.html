Terms & boundaries

Practical planning and organization support.

Highway 38 Solutions provides planning, cleanup, organization, concept, and workflow outputs. Final execution remains with the customer and qualified professionals when required.

Service scope

Services may include project planning, shop layout review, business cleanup, digital setup, file cleanup, custom internal tools, and Shop / CNC planning.

Not licensed professional work

Highway 38 Solutions does not provide licensed engineering, architectural, legal, tax, permit, electrical, machine guarding, safety certification, or code-compliance signoff unless a properly qualified professional is separately engaged.

Shop / CNC / automation boundary

Shop / CNC concept work is for planning, scoping, quote preparation, workflow review, ROI questions, fixture/inspection thinking, and vendor communication. Final robot programming, controls, electrical work, guarding, risk assessment, installation, and acceptance validation belong with qualified vendors/professionals.

Customer responsibility

  • Review all assumptions before acting.
  • Confirm measurements, site conditions, prices, vendor details, and safety requirements.
  • Use qualified contractors or professionals when required.
  • Do not rely on planning material as final permit, safety, engineering, legal, or tax advice.

Pricing and turnaround

Published ranges are planning ranges. Final price and turnaround depend on scope, missing information, file condition, revision needs, and requested deliverables.

Examples

Public examples may be demo/test examples unless clearly marked as real customer work. Real customer material should only be displayed with permission.
