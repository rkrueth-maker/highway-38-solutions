Frequently Asked Questions

Does a request automatically schedule work?

No. Every request is reviewed before a quote, schedule, charge or customer message is created.

Can customers review quotes online?

Yes. The Customer Portal supports quotes, invoices, messages, documents, photos, approvals and history after secure account activation.

What services are offered?

Snow and ice, lawn care, landscaping, excavation and grading, materials delivery and approved equipment service.

What equipment imagery is approved?

Chevrolet Duramax trucks and Boss snow plows are the approved public standards.

How is customer information handled?

Real customer information must use the dedicated Northern Lakes authenticated storage and portal deployment, not the browser-only preview database.
