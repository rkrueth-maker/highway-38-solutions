Privacy Notice

Northern Lakes Property Services collects information customers provide for service requests, estimates, property work, billing, documents, photos and account support.

Use of information

Information is used to review requests, prepare quotes, coordinate approved services, maintain business records and support customer accounts. It is not sold.

Approvals and external actions

Submitting a request does not authorize a charge, schedule, message or other external action. Owner approval controls remain in place.

Beta limitation

The public browser-only test interface must not be used for sensitive information until dedicated authentication and storage are verified.

Retention and access

Records are retained according to business, contractual, tax and legal requirements. Customers may request correction of account information.
