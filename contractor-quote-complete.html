                DIY proof checklist

                • Photograph existing conditions, utility marks, measurements, and access before work.
                • Save permits, product data, receipts, delivery tickets, warranties, and equipment rental records.
                • Photograph layout, hidden or concealed work, inspections, test results, finished work, and cleanup.
                • Record changes from the original plan, why they occurred, who approved them, and their cost or schedule effect.
                • Use the finished record to create the punch list, maintenance plan, warranty file, and future project history.
