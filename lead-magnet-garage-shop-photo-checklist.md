# Garage / Shop Photo Checklist

Take these photos before requesting a layout or shop review.

## Wide photos
- Stand at each corner and take one wide photo.
- Take one photo from each doorway or overhead door.

## Detail photos
- Workbench area
- Tool storage
- Parts or material storage
- Electrical outlets
- Lighting
- Walk paths
- Vehicle or equipment clearance
- Problem pile or blocked area

## Notes to include
- What feels slow or frustrating?
- What needs to stay?
- What can move?
- What is the first outcome you want?
