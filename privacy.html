Privacy

How request information is handled.

Highway 38 Solutions needs rough details to build clear outputs. This page explains what may be collected and how it is used.

Information you may send

You may choose to send photos, screenshots, measurements, files, links, receipts, job notes, customer messages, shop/process notes, or other details through the request form or follow-up communication.

How it is used

  • To understand the request.
  • To identify missing information.
  • To prepare a plan, layout, checklist, tracker, concept packet, or other agreed output.
  • To communicate about scope, pricing, turnaround, and delivery.

File and photo handling

Photos, screenshots, and files are treated as customer-provided working material. Public examples should use demo content or customer-approved material only.

Third-party tools

The site may use Google Forms, Google Sheets, GitHub Pages, email, and other basic business tools to receive and organize requests.

No sensitive information request

Do not send passwords, payment card numbers, Social Security numbers, medical information, or other sensitive personal information through the public form.

Contact

Use the request form to ask about a project or to request removal of submitted material from active working files.
