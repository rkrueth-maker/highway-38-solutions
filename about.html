Manufacturing foundation

Two decades inside machining and automation.

The Owner spent 20 years in precision manufacturing, including work as Machining Supervisor and Automation Chair. He introduced CNC equipment and programming, performed quoting, modernized machining processes, and programmed and set up mills, lathes, and EDM equipment.

Programmed and maintained thousands of CNC jobs over many years, including standardized tooling, post-processor support, feeds and speeds, and repeatable programming practices.

That experience supports a grounded approach to cycle time, workholding, inspection, material handling, operator steps, ROI, vendor scope, and the difference between a promising idea and an implementable process.
