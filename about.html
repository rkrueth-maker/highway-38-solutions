About Highway 38

Practical problem solving from shop floor to small business systems.

Highway 38 Solutions exists for the messy middle: when a project, shop, customer process, file mess, quote path, or automation idea needs to be sorted into a usable next step.

Why this exists

Most people do not need a giant software platform or a long consulting engagement. They need someone to sort the details, spot the missing information, organize the next move, and hand back something they can actually use.

Messy details in. Finished product out.

Shop and manufacturing background

  • CNC machining and programming experience
  • CNC mills, lathes, EDM, tooling, fixtures, and setup thinking
  • Automation, robot tending, conveyors, sensors, vision, pneumatics, part handling, and machine-integration concepts
  • Shop workflow, 5S, staging, layout, and bottleneck review

Business and quoting background

  • Quoting, estimating, job details, and customer follow-up organization
  • Service menus, intake forms, trackers, dashboards, and simple operating systems
  • Automotive, junkyard, parts-finding, title/process cleanup, and practical field problem solving
  • Project planning for garages, shops, repairs, remodel-style work, and material lists

AI-supported, not AI-only

AI is used as a helper to organize, draft, summarize, and build repeatable templates. The value is still practical judgment: what matters, what is missing, what should happen first, and what needs a qualified professional.

How work is delivered

Highway 38 is built for photos, files, screenshots, written notes, email-style communication, and clear handoff documents. Most starter work does not require a phone call.

Boundaries

Highway 38 provides planning, organization, workflow review, concept planning, and decision support. It does not replace licensed engineering, architecture, electrical work, machine guarding/safety validation, legal/tax advice, or permit/code authority.
