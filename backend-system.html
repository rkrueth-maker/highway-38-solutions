Order flow

From messy request to finished output.

This is the path that keeps Highway 38 from selling vague advice.

1. IntakeCustomer sends photos, notes, links, screenshots, measurements, or workflow pain.
2. TriageRequest is routed to Project, Shop, Business, Digital, Cleanup, Custom, Shop / CNC, or AI workflow.
3. BuildThe output becomes a snapshot, packet, tracker, concept brief, checklist, or quote path.
4. QAGoal, assumptions, boundaries, missing info, links, and first action are checked.
5. HandoffCustomer receives the usable output and the next best move.
6. UpgradeOnly if useful, Highway 38 suggests a deeper packet, add-on, or custom build.
