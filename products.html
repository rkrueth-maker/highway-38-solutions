FAQ

Common questions before you send the mess.

What is the business name?

Highway 38 Solutions is the public brand. Highway 38 Solutions LLC can be used later if the business is formally organized that way.

Are the desks separate businesses?

No. Project Desk, Shop Desk, Business Desk, Digital Desk, and Cleanup Desk are website categories under Highway 38 Solutions.

Can I send old texts, screenshots, photos, or half-finished files?

Yes. That is the point. Start with the rough details you already have and get back a cleaner working output.

What is not included?

Highway 38 Solutions does not provide legal, tax, licensed engineering, permit, or trade advice. This is planning, organizing, workflow, setup, copy, and practical output work.

Not sure where to start?

Send the mess. Highway 38 Solutions can point it to the right desk, standard package, or scoped custom work build.
