# RangeRivet Works Business Plan

## Working Brand

**RangeRivet Works**

**Tagline:** Practical tools, plans, and systems for garages, shops, and small businesses.

RangeRivet Works is the working umbrella brand for the combined product, planning, job-tracking, AI workflow, and shop-process improvement business. ForgeIQ remains the existing Shopify/code/store asset for now and should not be fully rebranded until the RangeRivet Works name is verified through Minnesota SOS, domain availability, USPTO, Google search, and social handles.

---

## Core Concept

RangeRivet Works helps homeowners, garage owners, small shops, contractors, landscapers, snow plow operators, handyman services, fabrication shops, garage-based businesses, and small business owners organize their spaces, jobs, materials, quotes, photos, receipts, invoices, workflows, and repeat business tasks.

The brand should feel practical, mechanical, shop-capable, and easy to understand. It should not lead with software or AI. It should lead with practical help.

---

## Main Business Lanes

### 1. Shop Products

Garage, shop, storage, workbench, tool, and mechanic-focused products.

This is the Shopify/store side. ForgeIQ can remain the working Shopify backend until the public brand is finalized.

Potential products:

- Garage organizers
- Tool storage
- Workbench accessories
- Shop supplies
- Small workflow products
- Digital templates
- Planning checklists
- Job tracking templates

---

### 2. Plans & Layout Help

Photo-based planning for garages, home shops, small shops, and small projects.

Starter offers:

#### Garage / Shop Layout Review

**Price:** $75-$150

Deliverables:

- Review photos or rough sketch
- Tool/equipment placement suggestions
- Storage/workbench/workflow suggestions
- Safety and access notes
- Written layout plan

#### Project Plan + Material List

**Price:** $150-$300

Deliverables:

- Step-by-step build order
- Material list
- Tool list
- Cost-saving notes
- Job sequence/checklist

#### Small Shop Workflow Cleanup

**Price:** $250-$500

Deliverables:

- Current process review
- Bottleneck list
- Quoting/workflow suggestions
- Storage and handling ideas
- Before-you-spend-money improvement plan

---

### 3. Job Tracking Setup

Simple job organization for small businesses that are too small for complicated software.

Target users:

- Landscapers
- Snow plow operators
- Handyman services
- Junk removal/hauling
- Garage/mechanic side businesses
- Small contractors
- Small fabrication or repair shops

Offers:

#### Job Tracker Template

**Price:** $49-$99

A Google Sheet / Drive-based customer, job, photo, material, receipt, quote, invoice, and status tracker.

#### Job Tracker Setup

**Price:** $150-$300

Customized setup of the tracker for a specific business.

#### Job Tracking Cleanup

**Price:** $300-$750

Organize messy job information, old photos, notes, receipts, quotes, and invoices into a cleaner system.

Working asset:

- Google Sheet renamed to: **RangeRivet Works Job Tracker MVP**

---

### 4. AI Workflow Setup

Simple AI help for real business tasks.

Position this as practical workflow help, not advanced AI consulting.

**Price:** $150-$500

Deliverables:

- Review repeat tasks
- Identify where AI can help
- Reusable prompt templates
- Customer reply templates
- Quote draft templates
- Invoice note templates
- Job summary templates
- Product description prompts
- Email/message cleanup workflow
- Simple AI rules requiring owner review

Main promise:

AI drafts. The business owner reviews before sending or using anything.

---

### 5. Shop Process Improvement

Premium add-on services based on CNC, automation, material handling, quoting, and shop-floor experience.

#### Equipment Quote Review

**Price:** $100-$500

Review machine/tool/storage/automation quotes before the customer spends money.

Deliverables:

- Missing questions
- Layout/workflow concerns
- Hidden cost questions
- Follow-up items
- Proceed / pause / compare recommendation

#### Automation Opportunity Review

**Price:** $250-$750+

Review a process and identify simple automation or process changes before major spending.

#### Machine / Shop Process Monitoring Review

**Price:** $200-$600

Define what to track, downtime categories, utilization notes, log setup, and first improvement targets.

#### CNC & Fabrication Workflow Review

**Price:** $300-$1,000+

Review material flow, setup/changeover, tooling organization, fixture/jig concepts, process notes, and error reduction.

## Launch Plan

### Phase 1: Working Brand Setup

- Use RangeRivet Works as working umbrella brand
- Keep ForgeIQ Shopify store/backend unchanged for now
- Rename Google Sheet and visible internal text
- Update website draft/Copilot workspace wording
- Create separate RangeRivet email only after final name check

### Phase 2: Name Verification

Before LLC filing, domain purchase, or final Shopify rebrand, check:

1. Minnesota Secretary of State business search
2. USPTO trademark search
3. Domain availability
4. Google exact-match search
5. Facebook/YouTube/Pinterest/TikTok/social handles
6. Shopify store/domain options

### Phase 3: First Offers

Start with three easy-to-understand offers:

1. Garage / Shop Layout Review
2. Quote / Estimate Cleanup or Equipment Quote Review
3. Job Tracker Setup

### Phase 4: Productize

Turn repeatable work into:

- Job tracker template
- Quote cleanup checklist
- Equipment quote review checklist
- Garage layout intake form
- AI prompt pack
- Project plan checklist
