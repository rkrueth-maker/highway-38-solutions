## Why this fits the current business

This does not change Highway 38 into a machine integrator. It expands the existing “messy details in, finished product out” model into the shop/CNC/automation world:

- Customer sends messy machine/shop/process information.
- Highway 38 turns it into a concept, decision packet, quote-ready scope, ROI worksheet, or vendor question list.
- Customer uses the output to decide, get quotes, or avoid spending money on the wrong improvement.
