# RangeRivet Works Business Plan

## 1. Executive Summary

**RangeRivet Works** is a working umbrella brand for practical garage, shop, contractor, and small-business support. The business combines shop-focused products, photo-based planning services, job-tracking setup, AI workflow setup, and premium shop-process improvement services.

**Tagline:** Practical tools, plans, and systems for garages, shops, and small businesses.

The business should lead with simple, understandable customer outcomes:

- Plan a garage, shop, or project before spending money.
- Organize customers, jobs, photos, quotes, receipts, and invoices.
- Use AI safely for repeat business tasks.
- Improve shop workflow, equipment decisions, and process bottlenecks.
- Sell useful garage, shop, storage, and workflow products through Shopify.

RangeRivet Works should feel practical, mechanical, local-capable, and trustworthy. It should not sound like a pure software company or vague AI consulting brand.

---

## 2. Brand Position

RangeRivet Works helps homeowners, garage owners, small shops, contractors, landscapers, snow plow operators, handyman services, repair shops, garage-based side businesses, fabrication shops, and small business owners turn messy spaces and scattered information into practical plans and simple systems.

**Core promise:**

Send photos, notes, measurements, or job details. RangeRivet Works helps turn them into a practical plan, cleaner quote, better layout, useful tracker, or repeatable workflow.

**Brand voice:**

- Practical
- Straightforward
- Shop-smart
- Low-pressure
- Problem-solving focused
- Clear enough for homeowners and small contractors

## 3. Business Lanes

### Lane 1: Shop Products

This is the Shopify/product side of the business.

Products may include:

- Garage organizers
- Tool storage products
- Workbench accessories
- Shop supplies
- Mechanic-focused products
- Small workflow products
- Digital templates
- Job tracker templates
- Planning checklists

**Current rule:** ForgeIQ can remain the existing Shopify/code/store asset behind the scenes until the RangeRivet Works name is fully verified and the rebrand decision is final.

---

### Lane 2: Plans & Layout Help

Photo-based planning for garages, home shops, small shops, and small projects.

#### Garage / Shop Layout Review

**Price:** $75-$150

**Best for:** Homeowners, garage owners, hobby shops, mechanics, and small shops.

**Deliverables:**

- Review of photos, sketch, or measurements
- Tool/equipment placement suggestions
- Storage, workbench, and workflow suggestions
- Safety and access notes
- Written layout plan

#### Project Plan + Material List

**Price:** $150-$300

**Best for:** Shelving, workbenches, garage upgrades, shed/utility projects, and small builds.

**Deliverables:**

- Build order
- Material list
- Tool list
- Cost-saving notes
- Job sequence/checklist

#### Small Shop Workflow Cleanup

**Price:** $250-$500

**Best for:** Repair shops, garage businesses, small shops, CNC/home shops, resellers, and fabrication areas.

**Deliverables:**

- Current process review
- Bottleneck list
- Quoting/workflow suggestions
- Storage and handling ideas
- Before-you-spend-money improvement plan

---

### Lane 3: Job Tracking Setup

Simple job organization for small businesses that are too small for complicated software.

**Target customers:**

- Landscapers
- Snow plow operators
- Handyman services
- Junk removal/hauling
- Garage/mechanic side businesses
- Small contractors
- Small fabrication or repair shops

#### Job Tracker Template

**Price:** $49-$99

A Google Sheet / Drive-based customer, job, photo, material, receipt, quote, invoice, and status tracker.

#### Job Tracker Setup

**Price:** $150-$300

Customized setup of the tracker for a specific business.

#### Job Tracking Cleanup

**Price:** $300-$750

Organize messy job information, old photos, notes, receipts, quotes, and invoices into a cleaner system.

**Working asset:** RangeRivet Works Job Tracker MVP.

---

### Lane 4: AI Workflow Setup

Simple AI help for real business tasks. This should be sold as practical workflow setup, not advanced AI consulting.

**Price:** $150-$500

**Best for:** Small businesses that want to save time but do not know where to start with AI.

**Deliverables:**

- Review of repeat tasks
- AI use-case list
- Reusable prompt templates
- Customer reply templates
- Quote draft templates
- Invoice note templates
- Job summary templates
- Product description prompts
- Email/message cleanup workflow
- Simple owner-review rules

**Main rule:** AI drafts. The business owner reviews before sending or using anything.

---

### Lane 5: Shop Process Improvement

Premium add-on services based on CNC, automation, material handling, quoting, equipment, and shop-floor experience.

#### Equipment Quote Review

**Price:** $100-$500

Review machine, tool, storage, lift, compressor, rack, CNC, or automation quotes before a customer spends money.

**Deliverables:**

- Missing questions
- Layout/workflow concerns
- Hidden cost questions
- Follow-up items
- Proceed / pause / compare recommendation

#### Automation Opportunity Review

**Price:** $250-$750+

Review a process and identify simple automation or process changes before major spending.

#### Machine / Shop Process Monitoring Review

**Price:** $200-$600

Define what to track, downtime categories, utilization notes, basic log setup, and first improvement targets.

#### CNC & Fabrication Workflow Review

**Price:** $300-$1,000+

Review material flow, setup/changeover, tooling organization, fixture/jig concepts, process notes, and error reduction.

## 5. Proof-of-Concept Stack

Use free or low-cost tools first:

- GitHub Pages for landing page
- Google Form or email intake for requests
- Google Sheets for job tracking MVP
- Google Drive for file/photo organization
- Shopify for product/store testing

Do not build a full app first. Build the app only after the Google Sheet workflow proves customers understand and use the service.

---

## 6. First 30-Day Launch Plan

### Week 1: Brand and Page

- Use RangeRivet Works as the working brand.
- Publish a static landing page.
- Keep ForgeIQ running behind the scenes.
- Use the RangeRivet Works Job Tracker MVP sheet.
- Do not file LLC or buy domain until name is checked.

### Week 2: Local Test Offers

Offer discounted test versions of:

- Garage / Shop Layout Review
- Job Tracker Setup
- Equipment Quote Review or Quote Cleanup

Goal: get the first 3-5 real conversations.

### Week 3: Sample Deliverables

Create sample outputs:

- One garage layout review sample
- One project plan + material list sample
- One job tracker setup sample
- One quote/equipment review sample
- One AI prompt pack sample

### Week 4: Decide What Sells Fastest

Track which offer gets the clearest interest:

- Layout help
- Job tracking setup
- Equipment/quote review
- AI workflow setup
- Shop process review

Then turn the winner into a repeatable package.

---

## 7. Naming and Legal Caution

RangeRivet Works is only the working name until verified.

Before filing an LLC, buying a domain, or fully rebranding Shopify, check:

1. Minnesota Secretary of State business search
2. USPTO trademark search
3. Domain availability
4. Google exact-match search
5. Social handles
6. Shopify store/domain options

---

Use RangeRivet Works for planning, site draft, and internal structure now. Keep ForgeIQ as the existing Shopify/backend asset until the name is verified and the rebrand decision is final.
