## Brand Meaning

Highway 38 Solutions is a practical planning, cleanup, layout, and systems business for homeowners, garages, small shops, and small businesses.

The business helps customers turn messy ideas into clear plans, layouts, quotes, systems, and next steps.

Highway 38 has a local northern Minnesota feel and is broad enough to cover:

- Home projects
- Garage layouts
- Shop layouts
- Repair-shop planning
- Welding-shop planning later
- Project plans
- Material lists
- Quote sheets
- Small business cleanup
- Websites
- AI setup
- Customer/job trackers
- File/photo/receipt cleanup
- Sprinkler/camera/property layouts
- Workflow improvement

## Simple Package Names

### Problem Snapshot

Small problem review.

Customer sends photos, notes, or screenshots. Highway 38 sends back a clear written recommendation.

### Project Packet

Full project plan.

Includes layout ideas, material list, build order, notes, and next steps.

### Shop Flow Review

Garage/shop/workspace improvement.

Includes tool placement, workflow, storage, safety/access notes, and layout suggestions.

### Business Cleanup Packet

Small business organization.

Includes service menu, quote sheet, customer intake form, tracker, and follow-up system.

### Digital Setup Packet

Website/AI/form setup.

Includes website outline, page copy, AI prompts, form layout, and basic automation plan.

### Custom Work Build

Scoped custom output.

Used when the standard packages are close but not exact.
