Clear Boundaries

Practical planning and setup help, not licensed trade work

Northwoods focuses on organization, planning, workflow, simple websites, trackers, forms, quote tools, and owner-ready next steps.

✅

What this is

Planning help, simple webpages, app-style tools, checklists, trackers, workflow drafts, quote organization, and setup recommendations.

⚠️

What this is not

Not permit-ready structural, electrical, plumbing, HVAC, irrigation engineering, legal, insurance, tax, or financial work.

AI-assisted work is drafted and organized, but the owner reviews before sending, buying, building, trenching, quoting, billing, installing, or publishing.
