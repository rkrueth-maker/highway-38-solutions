Clear Boundaries

Practical planning help, not licensed trade work

Northwoods Problem Solvers focuses on organization, planning, workflow, and owner-ready next steps.

✅

What this is

Planning help, layout concepts, checklists, trackers, workflow drafts, customer/job tracking, quote organization, sprinkler layout concepts, and setup recommendations.

⚠️

What this is not

Not permit-ready structural, electrical, plumbing, HVAC, irrigation engineering, legal, insurance, tax, or financial work.

👀

Owner review required

AI-assisted work is drafted and organized, but the owner reviews before sending, buying, building, trenching, quoting, billing, or installing.

Note: If a project requires a licensed contractor, electrician, plumber, irrigation contractor, engineer, inspector, attorney, accountant, insurance agent, or other professional, use the proper licensed help.
