Can I send old texts, screenshots, or half-finished files?

Yes. That is the point. ForgeIQ starts with the rough details you already have.

Will ForgeIQ pick software for me?

Only if the workflow proves you need it. The first goal is a useful organized output.

How does Custom Work start?

You send the rough problem first. Then you get a scope note, delivery format, quote, and boundary check.

Not legal, tax, permit, licensed engineering, or trade advice. This is planning, organizing, workflow, setup, copy, and practical output work.
