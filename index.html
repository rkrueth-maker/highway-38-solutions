Clear Boundaries

Practical planning help, not licensed trade work

RangeRivet focuses on organization, planning, workflow, and owner-ready next steps.

✅

What this is

Planning help, layout concepts, checklists, trackers, workflow drafts, quote organization, sprinkler layout concepts, and setup recommendations.

⚠️

What this is not

Not permit-ready structural, electrical, plumbing, HVAC, irrigation engineering, legal, or financial work.

👀

Owner review required

AI-assisted work is drafted and organized, but the owner reviews before sending, buying, building, trenching, or installing.

Note: If a project requires a licensed contractor, electrician, plumber, irrigation contractor, engineer, inspector, attorney, accountant, or other professional, use the proper licensed help. Sprinkler planning should be verified against water pressure, flow rate, backflow requirements, utility marking, and local rules before buying or installing.
