FAQ

Clear expectations make the first request easier.

What should I send?

Photos, notes, screenshots, measurements, links, customer/job details, current forms/sheets, and the outcome you want.

What if I do not know what I need?

Start with a Project Cleanup Snapshot. It sorts the details and points to the next best service.

Can we start without a call?

Yes. The easiest start is form, email, text, photos, and documents.

Is this licensed trade work?

No. This is planning, setup, organization, and workflow help. Licensed trade, legal, tax, insurance, or engineering work needs the proper professional.

What this is

Planning help, webpages, app-style tools, checklists, trackers, workflow drafts, quote organization, and setup recommendations.

What this is not

Not permit-ready structural, electrical, plumbing, HVAC, irrigation engineering, legal, insurance, tax, or financial work.

AI-assisted work is drafted and organized, but the owner reviews before sending, buying, building, trenching, quoting, billing, installing, or publishing.
