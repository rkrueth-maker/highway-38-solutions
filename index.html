Store Direction

ForgeIQ / Shopify Store: parked until traffic or demand proves it

The store can wait. The smarter move is to sell services first, learn what people actually ask for, then turn the winning service outputs into templates, trackers, digital products, or tool kits later.

Test a Service First
  • Do now: sell snapshots, webpages, forms, trackers, and quote tools.
  • Watch for demand: repeated requests for the same tracker, checklist, or template.
  • Build later: sell proven templates through ForgeIQ or Shopify after traffic exists.
  • Rule: do not spend store time before one service offer gets real interest.
