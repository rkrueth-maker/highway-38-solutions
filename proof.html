Proof & Control

Clear labels. Human approval. No invented claims.

Highway 38 separates verified proof from representative examples, keeps assumptions visible, and preserves customer and owner control over external actions.

View ExamplesStart a Project

Proof classifications

Know exactly what you are looking at.

Verified Proof

A real completed result supported by approved evidence. Any customer identity, result statement, image, or measurement must be authorized and accurately represented.

Representative Example

Imagery or content used to explain an environment, workflow, or possible deliverable. It is not presented as Highway 38 customer work.

Hypothetical Demonstration

Sample information used to demonstrate structure, workflow, software, plans, or documents. It does not promise the same result for a real project.

Approval system

Important actions stop for review.

Scope and price

The customer approves what is included, excluded, assumed, priced, and required before work begins.

Customer-facing actions

Quotes, proposals, major changes, publishing, sharing, scheduling, purchasing, and final delivery remain approval-gated.

Professional verification

Survey, engineering, permit, electrical, guarding, safety, code, and final-pricing triggers stay visible.

Customer control

Nothing important happens silently.

No automatic charge

A request does not create a purchase, invoice, subscription, or work order.

No automatic send

Customer, vendor, and public messages remain controlled unless explicitly approved.

Data isolation

Business records, users, private pricing, files, and configurations remain separated.

Recovery evidence

Proof, errors, backups, change history, and rollback information are preserved where applicable.
