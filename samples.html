What the work can look like

These examples are intentionally simple. The goal is to show clear output, not overbuild a complicated system before a customer asks for it.

1. Project Cleanup Snapshot Sample

Example customer: Pine Ridge Property Help has messy notes about a garage cleanup, camera placement, and a small landing page idea.

Customer Sends

  • 6 garage photos
  • Rough 24' x 28' measurement
  • List of tools and storage issues
  • Notes from text messages
  • Goal: make the garage usable and explain services online

Northwoods Delivers

  • Plain-English problem summary
  • Missing measurements and photo list
  • Risk check: electrical, trip hazards, weather exposure
  • Simple phase plan: clear, measure, layout, quote, build
  • Next-step checklist the owner can act on

4. Google Form + Tracker Demo

The form collects details. The tracker turns them into work that can be reviewed, quoted, followed up, or archived.

Form Fields

  • Name and contact method
  • Service needed
  • Photos / links
  • Budget range
  • Deadline or timing
  • What outcome they want

Tracker Columns

  • Status
  • Priority
  • Quoted price
  • Follow-up date
  • Next action
  • Internal notes

6. Garage / Yard / Sprinkler Planning Sample

Photos, satellite screenshots, rough dimensions, and goals can be organized into a practical owner-ready planning packet.

Inputs

Photos, satellite screenshot, rough measurements, water source location, driveway, trees, fence, tools, goals.

Output

Layout concept, missing measurements, risk notes, suggested zones or work areas, next-step checklist.

Boundary

Planning only. Licensed work, permits, inspections, pressure/flow verification, and code compliance must be handled by the proper professional.
