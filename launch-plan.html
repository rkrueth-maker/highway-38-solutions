Go-live plan

Launch-ready checklist for tomorrow.

This page keeps the final launch tasks visible: domain, forms, sitemap, legal pages, test requests, analytics, and final QA.

Prioritized launch checklist

Do P0 first. P1 next. P2 after launch.

The site can go live once P0 is checked and one full test request is run.

P0 — launch blockers

Must be done before public URL

  • Confirm final public URL / custom domain.
  • Confirm GitHub Pages source and SSL.
  • Submit one test request through the live Google Form.
  • Confirm owner alert email and customer confirmation email.
  • Open homepage on mobile and verify menu works.
  • Open Packages, Shop / CNC, Examples, Backend, Privacy, Terms.
  • Confirm sitemap and robots use final URL.
  • Confirm form links are not mailto links.
P1 — high confidence polish

Strongly recommended before sharing

  • Run H38-T001 test order end-to-end.
  • Run H38-A001 test order end-to-end.
  • Replace any internal/recovery language on public pages.
  • Check all CTAs point to the live form.
  • Add custom domain to GitHub Pages.
  • Add Google Search Console after domain is active.
  • Add simple analytics or click tracking if desired.
  • Use the site on phone, Chromebook, and desktop.
P2 — after launch

Improvements after first traffic

  • Add real customer-approved before/after photos.
  • Turn test deliverables into downloadable samples.
  • Add FAQ page.
  • Add short homepage proof video or screenshot strip.
  • Create one lead magnet: Idle Reason Tracker or Project Checklist.
  • Create Highway 38 Supply Co. link when store is ready.
  • Review pricing after first 3–5 real leads.

Launch rule

Do not wait for perfect. Launch after one successful form submission and one successful sample deliverable test.
